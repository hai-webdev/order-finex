WHO WE ARE.

FINEX is a small team of Portland based craftspeople, cooks and designers dedicated to crafting cookware that will stand the test of time.

Our work is inspired by the hardworking history of American cast iron and grounded in our belief that cooking should be genuine—an experience you value and share with others. It’s these simple ideals that guide us as we reinvent cast iron cookware with modern design to enhance the life of every cook.
